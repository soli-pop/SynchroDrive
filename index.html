The Origin

The car speaks.
Through vibration, resistance,
sound, and balance — it
constantly communicates.

Most drivers force it forward. SYNCHODRIVE learned to listen. Long before the team had a name, there were drivers who noticed something others ignored — the machine is not a tool to be conquered, but a partner to be understood.

From this revelation, founder Azarias Vetrovsky built a philosophy: driving is about synchronization, awareness, and respect — treating the machine not as something to dominate, but as an extension of the body itself.

Azarias Vetrovsky
Team Captain & Founder

Tempered by loss and resilience, Azarias came to understand the fragility of life and the importance of respect — toward people, machines, and the forces that drive them. His belief: true mastery comes not from domination, but from harmony and connection.

Operations Manual

Team Protocols

📅
Weekly Training Schedule
Driver Training runs 1–2 times per week — on-track or simulator sessions focused on synchronization, precision, and rhythm exercises. Optional personal development including fitness and mental training is encouraged but not mandatory.
🏁
Monthly Track Days
Full Track Day / Endurance Training held 1–2 days per month. Longer sessions to build stamina, test strategies, and enhance team coordination across extended high-speed runs.
🔧
Vehicle Maintenance
One dedicated maintenance and testing session per month. Inspect, tune, and test vehicles for optimal performance. Drivers are encouraged to understand the mechanical systems of their assigned units.
🛠
Engineering Workshop
Monthly innovation workshop for brainstorming, testing new techniques, or experimenting with vehicle modifications and engineering upgrades aligned with the team's philosophy.
📊
Performance Review
Monthly performance review and goal-setting session. Evaluate individual and team progress, analyze telemetry data, and set measurable targets for the upcoming cycle.
🤝
Team Building
Two team-building activities per month — collaborative exercises, kart racing, escape rooms, or social outings designed to strengthen trust, communication, and collective harmony.
📢
Community Events
Optional 1–2 community or promotional events per month. Engage with Synchers, participate in exhibitions, or host demonstration events to promote the SYNCHODRIVE philosophy to the public.
🎯
Strategy Sessions
Monthly team briefing and strategy sessions. Discuss upcoming race events, review performance data, analyze competitor profiles, and plan tactical improvements for each vehicle unit.
